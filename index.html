How it works

A proper clean, not just a quick once-over

Most carpet cleaning is a basic rinse. {{ site.business_name }} focuses on a thorough clean with the right treatment for the material, the level of soiling and the type of problem areas.

1

Inspection & pre-treatment

We inspect the carpet or upholstery first, identify problem areas, and apply the right pre-treatment to loosen built-up dirt, spots and traffic marks.

2

Deep cleaning & fibre agitation

Where needed, fibres are worked properly so the cleaning process reaches deeper into the pile, lifting out more soil than a surface clean.

3

Rinse, extraction & fresh finish

The carpet or upholstery is thoroughly cleaned and extracted — removing loosened dirt, improving appearance and leaving everything fresher and quicker to dry.
