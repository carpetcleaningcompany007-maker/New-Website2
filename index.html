Our cleaning process

A proper clean, not just a quick once over

A lot of carpet cleaning is just a basic rinse. {{ site.business_name }} focuses on giving carpets and upholstery a more thorough clean with the right treatment for the material, the level of soiling and the type of problem areas involved.

1

Inspection and pre treatment

We inspect the carpet or upholstery first, identify the main problem areas, and apply the right pre treatment to loosen built up dirt, spots and traffic marks.

2

Deep cleaning and fibre agitation

Where needed, fibres are worked properly so the cleaning process can reach deeper into the pile, helping lift out more soil than a quick surface clean.

3

Rinse, extraction and fresh finish

The carpet or upholstery is then thoroughly cleaned and extracted, helping remove loosened dirt, improve appearance and leave everything fresher and quicker drying.
