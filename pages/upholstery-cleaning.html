Our process

How we clean upholstery properly

A careful step by step process for cleaner fresher fabric seating.

1

Inspect and test

We check the fabric first, look at the soiling and identify the safest and most suitable cleaning approach before any wet work begins.

2

Pre treat and work through the fabric

Targeted solution is applied to the main build up areas so body oils, arm marks and everyday grime begin to break down properly.

3

Rinse and extract

The loosened soil is extracted out, helping leave the upholstery cleaner, fresher and easier to enjoy again.
