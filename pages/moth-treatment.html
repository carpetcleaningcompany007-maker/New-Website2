Service Overview

Moth Treatment

Moth activity in carpets and rugs is one of those problems many people only notice once damage has started to appear. JW Carpet Care offers moth treatment support and carpet related advice for homes across Worcestershire and the West Midlands where customers want a practical, informed response rather than guesswork.

This service is particularly relevant for wool carpets, rugs and areas of reduced foot traffic where larvae may go unnoticed for longer. A proper visit allows the issue to be looked at in context, the likely activity discussed and the next step explained clearly.

Moth related work is different from a general clean. It requires care, the right process and a more specialist conversation around what is active, what is old damage and what can be done to reduce the chance of further problems. For homes with valuable wool flooring or rugs, that kind of practical guidance matters.
