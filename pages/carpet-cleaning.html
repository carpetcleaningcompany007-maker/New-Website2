Our process

How we clean carpets properly

A proper step by step cleaning process for a cleaner, fresher and more presentable result.

1

Inspect and pre treat

We look at the carpet first, identify the main soil build up, marks and traffic lanes, then apply the right treatment so the soil starts breaking down before extraction begins.

2

Work through the pile

Agitation helps lift dirt from deep within the fibres. This is what stops the clean being just a quick surface rinse.

3

Rinse and extract

Hot water extraction flushes loosened soil out properly and leaves the carpet cleaner, fresher and far easier to maintain.
