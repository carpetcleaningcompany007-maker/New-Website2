Our carpet cleaning process

Truck mounted carpet cleaning with a professional step by step system

This is where the difference is made. The cleaning process is designed to break down soil properly, work through the carpet fibres and then extract dirt at high temperature using powerful equipment.

Pre vacuum

Dry soil and loose debris are removed first so grit is not left sitting in the carpet before wet cleaning begins.

Targeted pre spray

A suitable pre spray is applied to break down traffic lane soiling, greasy build up and everyday dirt within the fibres.

CRB agitation

A counter rotating brush machine works the solution through the carpet pile, separates the fibres and helps release stubborn dirt.

Truck mounted hot water extraction

High temperature hot water extraction with the truck mounted system flushes out loosened soil and leaves the carpet cleaner, fresher and properly rinsed.
