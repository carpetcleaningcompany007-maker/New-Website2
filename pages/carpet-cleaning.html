Carpet cleaning that makes a visible difference

Not every carpet needs replacing

A lot of carpets look tired because of built up soil, greasy traffic lanes, spills, pet mess and general everyday use. In many cases, the carpet itself still has plenty of life left in it. It just needs the right cleaning process.

{{ site.business_name }} provides professional carpet cleaning across {{ site.service_area }} using a proper step by step approach designed to break down soil, work through the fibres and extract the dirt out properly, leaving the carpet cleaner, fresher and far more presentable.

Whether you are trying to freshen up a home, improve a rental property or get on top of heavily used family areas, the aim is simple: get the carpet looking and feeling right again.

Our process

How we clean carpets properly

A proper step by step cleaning process for a cleaner, fresher and more presentable result.

1. Inspect and pre treat

We look at the carpet first, identify traffic areas, marks and heavier build up, then apply the right pre treatment to start loosening the dirt.

2. Work through the pile

Agitation helps distribute the solution through the fibres, lift the pile and release the dirt that a quick surface clean often leaves behind.

3. Rinse and extract

The carpet is then thoroughly cleaned and extracted to remove loosened soil, leaving it cleaner, fresher and much more presentable.

Why choose us

A stronger result than a basic rinse only clean

Some carpet cleaning is little more than a quick wet pass over the surface. That can freshen things slightly, but it often does not go deep enough to tackle the real build up sitting in the pile.

Using pre treatment, agitation and a proper rinse and extraction process gives a far better chance of improving appearance, freshness and overall feel underfoot.
