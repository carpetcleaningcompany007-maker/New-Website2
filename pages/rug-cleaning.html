Service Overview

Rug Cleaning

Rugs often carry some of the heaviest everyday use in the home. They take traffic, food drops, pet marks and constant wear while also acting as a visual centrepiece. JW Carpet Care provides professional rug cleaning across Worcestershire and the West Midlands to help rugs look fresher, cleaner and better cared for.

Many rugs can be cleaned effectively in the home, depending on fibre type, backing and condition. Modern rugs, everyday area rugs and selected feature rugs can all benefit from proper cleaning when they begin to look dull or hold onto stale odours and general dirt.

Rug cleaning works especially well when booked alongside carpet cleaning or upholstery cleaning because it lifts the whole room at the same time. If there are marks requiring extra attention, stain removal treatments can also be discussed as part of the job.
