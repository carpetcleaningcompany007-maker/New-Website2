Stain removal that tackles the real problem

Some marks need more than a general clean

A general carpet or upholstery clean can improve a lot, but some stains need more focused treatment. That is especially true with drink spills, pet accidents, food marks, old traffic lanes and previous DIY attempts that have left residues behind.

JW Carpet Care provides targeted stain removal across Worcestershire and the West Midlands using the right spot treatment process first, followed by proper rinsing and extraction where needed. The aim is always to improve the mark as far as reasonably possible without making false promises.

Whether the issue is one stubborn patch or several problem areas, the goal is the same: improve the appearance, reduce the distraction and leave the area cleaner and more presentable.

Our process

How we approach stain removal properly

A focused process designed to improve the problem area as far as reasonably possible.

1

Identify the stain type

We look at what the mark is likely to be, how long it has been there and how the fibre may react before treatment starts.

2

Apply the right treatment

Targeted spot treatment is used to start breaking down the stain so it has a better chance of improving properly.

3

Rinse and recover the area

The treated area is flushed through and extracted where needed to leave it cleaner, fresher and easier to blend back in.

Why it works

A better chance of improvement than a standard clean alone

Not every stain comes out completely, but the right treatment gives you a far better chance of improvement than treating every mark the same way.
